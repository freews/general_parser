#### 6.2.2 Incomplete/Unsuccessful Shutdown

> **Section ID**: 6.2.2 | **Page**: 75-76

An incomplete/unsuccessful shutdown is a Normal or Abrupt power down that did not complete 100%
of the shutdown sequence for any reason that causes the device to be unable to guarantee data/metadata
integrity (e.g., firmware hang/crash, capacitor failure, PLP circuit failure, etc.).

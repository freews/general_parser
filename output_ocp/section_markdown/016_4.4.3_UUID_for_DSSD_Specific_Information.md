#### 4.4.3 UUID for DSSD Specific Information

> **Section ID**: 4.4.3 | **Page**: 9-9

A UUID has been defined for use in commands to ensure that the vendor specific Log Identifiers and
Feature Identifiers used in this specification access the functionality defined in this specification (i.e., do
not access other vendor specific functionality that may use the same vendor specific identifiers).

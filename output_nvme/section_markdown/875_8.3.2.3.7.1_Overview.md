###### 8.3.2.3.7.1 Overview

> **Section ID**: 8.3.2.3.7.1 | **Page**: 718-718

ZoneAlias members are represented as type-length-value (TLV) data structures. ZoneAlias members may
be any of the Zone member types specified in Figure 742 except the ZoneAlias Zone member type (i.e.,
ZMTYPE field set to EFh).

##### 5.3.1.2 RecommendedAccessGranularity

> **Section ID**: 5.3.1.2 | **Page**: 99-99

5.3.1.2 RecommendedAccessGranularity 
This column is used to report the granularity that the Storage Device recommends when the host invokes the Set or 
Get method on byte tables. 
This column SHALL NOT be modifiable by the host.
